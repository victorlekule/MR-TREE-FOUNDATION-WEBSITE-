Frequently Asked Questions.

Everything you need to know about giving your time or resources to Mr Tree Foundation.

Donation Questions

Where exactly does my money go?
100% of public donations are strictly allocated to our field projects. This includes purchasing indigenous tree saplings, funding clean ethanol cookstoves for rural families, and equipping our Lake Victoria cleanup crews.
Is my online payment secure?
Absolutely. We do not store any of your payment information. All card and mobile money transactions are heavily encrypted and processed through industry-leading gateways like PesaPal, Stripe, and PayPal.
Can I donate using M-Pesa or Tigo Pesa?
Yes! When you select the "PesaPal / Mobile" option in our donation form, you will be able to easily complete your contribution using local Tanzanian mobile money networks.
Will I receive a receipt for tax purposes?
Yes, a detailed receipt will automatically be sent to the email address you provide during checkout. If you represent a corporation requiring specific documentation, please contact our HQ directly.

Volunteer Questions

Do I need prior environmental experience?
Not at all! We welcome passionate individuals from all backgrounds. Our field leaders provide complete, on-the-spot training for tree planting, safe waste removal, and community engagement before every event.
What should I bring to a field event?
We provide the tools (shovels, gloves, trash bags). You should bring a reusable water bottle, wear comfortable closed-toe shoes, and apply sun protection. We will give you a Mr Tree Foundation high-vis vest upon arrival.
Are there age restrictions for volunteers?
Our general field events require volunteers to be at least 16 years old. However, children are welcome to participate in community education days and specific nursery events if accompanied by a parent or guardian.
Can my company or school organize a group?
Yes! We love hosting corporate teams and student organizations. Simply fill out the volunteer form and note your group size in the "Message" section. Our coordinator will contact you to arrange a dedicated impact day.
