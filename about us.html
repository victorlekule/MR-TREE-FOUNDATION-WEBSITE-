Who We Are

We are the
Mr Tree Foundation

Mr Tree Foundation is a premier environmental non-governmental organization headquartered in Mwanza, Tanzania. Established by a collective of passionate youth, environmental scientists, and community leaders, we recognized a critical need to combat rapid deforestation and environmental degradation in the Lake Zone.

We do not simply plant trees; we cultivate a culture of sustainability. By integrating modern ecological science with traditional community knowledge, we ensure that every initiative we launch takes deep root and provides lasting benefits for both the planet and the people.

Why We Exist

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower Tanzanian communities through actionable environmental education, sustainable forestry, and climate-resilient economic opportunities, ensuring the protection of biodiversity for current and future generations.

Our Vision

To see a thriving, green Tanzania where ecosystems and communities coexist in perfect harmony, leading East Africa in ecological restoration and sustainable circular economies.

Our Core Values

The guiding principles that dictate our behavior and drive every project we undertake.

Sustainability

Every action we take is designed to have a long-term, positive environmental footprint.

Inclusivity

We believe environmental protection is everyone's duty, regardless of background or status.

Integrity

We operate with complete transparency, honoring our commitments to donors and nature alike.

Innovation

We constantly seek new, data-driven approaches to solve complex environmental challenges.

Our Strategic Goals

The actionable steps we take to protect Tanzania's natural heritage.

Community Driven

Empowering locals to take charge of their environment through education and resources.

Clean Energy Strategies

Strengthening environmental conservation strategies through promoting the use of affordable and clean energy.

Knowledge Creation

Supporting knowledge creation on green mining, climate change adaptation, afforestation, Agroforestry and related programs.

Poverty Reduction

Empowering education on entrepreneurship with the aim of reducing poverty.

National Policy Support

Playing a vital role in developing and promoting National Environmental Policies.

Circular Economy

Emphasizing and enhancing sustainable waste circular economy for the local and national levels.
