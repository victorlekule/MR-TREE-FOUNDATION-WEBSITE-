Questions about Donating?

Everything you need to know about transparency, receipts, and allocation.

How do I notify you after making a direct bank transfer?
Once you have completed your wire transfer, please send an email to finance@mrtreefoundation.org with your transaction reference number or a screenshot of the receipt. We will confirm receipt and send your official documentation.
Will I receive a receipt for tax purposes?
Yes. Mr Tree Foundation is a registered NGO in Tanzania. We provide official stamped receipts for all direct transfers and mobile money donations. If you are donating internationally, please consult your local tax authority regarding deductions.
Can I direct my funds to a specific project?
Absolutely. If you want your $500 (or any amount) to go exclusively to Tree Planting, Cookstoves, or Lake Cleanup, simply include that note in your transfer reference or in your follow-up email to us. We honor all donor requests.
Do you accept checks?
Yes, we accept physical checks made payable to "Mr Tree Foundation". You can drop them off at our headquarters in Nyamagana, Mwanza, or contact us for our postal mailing address.
