Who We Are

Rooted in Tanzania.
Growing the Future.

Mr Tree Foundation is a dynamic environmental organization dedicated to transforming Tanzania. We combine cutting-edge ecological practices with passionate community action to restore our nation's natural world.

Community Driven Empowering locals to take charge of their environment through education and resources.

Clean Energy Strategies Strengthening conservation strategies by promoting the use of affordable and clean energy.

Knowledge Creation Supporting knowledge creation on green mining, climate adaptation, afforestation, and agroforestry.

Poverty Reduction Empowering education on entrepreneurship with the aim of reducing poverty nationwide.

National Policy Support Playing a key role in developing and promoting Tanzania's National Environmental Policies.

Circular Economy Emphasizing sustainable waste management for both local communities and national levels.

Our Approach

How We Take Action

We don't just plant trees; we cultivate ecosystems of change through education, advocacy, and direct community involvement.

Environmental Education

We conduct workshops, school programs, and community training to raise awareness about sustainable practices and equip the next generation of leaders.

Community Engagement

We work directly alongside local communities to implement sustainable solutions that are carefully tailored to their specific needs and economic realities.

Policy Advocacy

We are a voice for the environment, tirelessly advocating for responsible, data-backed ecological policies at both the local and national levels in Tanzania.

Creative Influence

We harness the power of media, arts, and cultural events to spread critical environmental messages in engaging, memorable, and inspiring ways.

Stronger Together

Backed by Visionaries.

Transforming the Lake Zone requires massive collaboration. We are proud to work alongside government bodies, global NGOs, and corporate leaders to scale our impact.
