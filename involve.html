Frequently Asked Questions.

Everything you need to know about partnering or volunteering with Mr Tree Foundation.

Partnership Questions

What types of organizations do you partner with?
We collaborate with corporations, local businesses, international NGOs, government agencies, and academic institutions that share our vision for a sustainable, green Tanzania.
How does corporate sponsorship work?
We offer highly customizable Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) packages. Your company can financially sponsor specific tree-planting events, clean-cookstove distributions, or massive lake cleanups, with measurable impact reports provided.
Can we partner through in-kind donations?
Absolutely. We welcome in-kind support such as providing planting tools, transport logistics, media coverage, safety gear, or technical expertise that amplify our field operations.
Will our logo be featured on your platforms?
Yes. Official partners and sponsors are prominently featured on our website's Partner Grid, in our annual impact reports, and across event banners and marketing materials, recognizing your commitment.

Volunteer Questions

Do I need prior environmental experience?
Not at all! We welcome passionate individuals from all backgrounds. Our field leaders provide complete, on-the-spot training for tree planting, safe waste removal, and community engagement before every event.
What should I bring to a field event?
We provide the tools (shovels, gloves, trash bags). You should bring a reusable water bottle, wear comfortable closed-toe shoes, and apply sun protection. We will give you a Mr Tree Foundation high-vis vest upon arrival.
Are there age restrictions for volunteers?
Our general field events require volunteers to be at least 16 years old. However, children are welcome to participate in community education days and specific nursery events if accompanied by a parent or guardian.
Can my company or school organize a group?
Yes! We love hosting corporate teams and student organizations. Simply fill out the volunteer form and note your group size in the "Message" section. Our coordinator will contact you to arrange a dedicated impact day.
